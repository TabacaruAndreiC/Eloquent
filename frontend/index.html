How to Use Speech Analyzer

📋 Record Live

  1. Click Start Recording
  2. Tell your speech
  3. Click Stop Recording
  4. Click Analyze Speech

📁 Upload Audio

  1. Click the menu icon (top-left)
  2. Select Add Audio
  3. Drag & drop or browse for a file
  4. Click Analyze Speech

📜 History

  1. Click the menu icon (top-left)
  2. Select History
  3. Click any recording to view its results
  4. Use 🗑️ to delete recordings

💡 Tips

  • Use a quality microphone
  • Quiet environment is best
  • Speak naturally
  • Supported formats: MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A, WEBM, WEBA, FLAC, AAC

🎯 What We Analyze

  • Filler words (um, uh, like)
  • Speech coherence
  • Vocabulary
  • Charisma Score (1-100)
